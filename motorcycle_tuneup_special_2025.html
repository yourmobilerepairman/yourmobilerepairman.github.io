Our basic service includes:

Fluids:
  • Oil and filter change: We use high-quality Castrol 10W-40 full synthetic motorcycle oil and a FRAM Ultra Synthetic oil filter (or equivalent) to keep your engine running smoothly.
  • Fluid level check: We'll top off essential fluids like brake fluid (using STP Synthetic DOT-4 brake fluid) and coolant (using UltraCool ULTRA PLUS1 Antifreeze Coolant).
    • Please note that full fluid changes will incur additional parts and labor costs.
Drive system:
  • Chain drive: We'll inspect your chain and sprockets, then clean, lubricate, and adjust the chain tension as needed.
  • Belt drive: We'll inspect, clean, and adjust the belt tension as needed.
  • Note: This service does not include shaft drive systems.
General inspection:
  • Tire pressure check and inspection: We'll ensure your tires are at the correct pressure for optimal handling and safety, and we'll check for any signs of wear or damage.
  • Battery check: We'll test your battery's health to ensure reliable starts every time.
  • General nut and bolt check: We'll give your bike a once-over to make sure everything is tight and secure.
  • Air filter inspection: We will inspect your air filter and let you know if it needs to be cleaned or replaced.
    • This service may or may not be included in the basic tune-up, depending on your motorcycle's make and model.
